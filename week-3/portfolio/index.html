Hi, I'm Kenia

Skillsets centering on Typography, UX, Production, and Motion. Focusing on building clear visual systems and experiences that feel intentional, accessible, and memorable. Whether it’s a screen, a printed piece, or something in motion, thinking about how real people will interact with the design is of large interest.

Design philosophy is to be clean and effective. Rooted in Bauhaus principles, strong typography, and thoughtful use of color. Understanding the core problem and the human experience behind it are key in designing toward clarity and impact. The goal is always the same: create work that not only functions well, but leaves people with a positive, lasting impression through design.
